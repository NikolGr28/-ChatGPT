Emily Evans

Emily Evans, with her striking hazel eyes and auburn curls cascading down her shoulders, exudes an air of mystery and charm. A renowned art curator, her elegant demeanor masks a complex past and hidden motives.
Despite her poised exterior, whispers of her involvement in the murder case linger, casting shadows over her impeccable reputation. Yet, beneath the facade lies a vulnerable soul, torn between loyalty and deception, leaving detectives like Liam Parker torn between suspicion and desire for the truth.
